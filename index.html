Teenynotes

A tiny browser-based word processor by Lorcan.

To begin writing, simply click anywhere in the text and type. Highlight a piece of text & try out the following keyboard shortcuts:

  • Ctrl + B = bold
  • Ctrl + I = italic
  • Ctrl + U = underline

That's all there is to it! When you're done, copy and paste it to whatever internet treasure chest you desire.

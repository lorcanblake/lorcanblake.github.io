Education

I'm currently a second year student of Creative Multimedia, a course run by Limerick School of Art & Design in Ireland.

I've gained exposure to many softwares, including Adobe's Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Fireworks and Flash, as well as Audacity, Reaper, and Brackets.

Language-wise, I'm comfortable with ActionScript and Javascript, as well as common Javascript and CSS frameworks.

Outside of college, I enjoy learning about new CSS and SVG browser technologies, as well as typography and design-thinking theory. I'm great at parties, I swear.

 

Principles

As much as I love the Internet, it's got problems. The average site takes an eternity to load, looks ugly and is annoying to use.

Learning from their mistakes, I create web applications that are pretty, performant and a joy to use.

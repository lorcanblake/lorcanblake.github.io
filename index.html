I wear many hats.

As a curious person, I've got experience in many disciplines — web and graphic design, web and game development, audio editing, social media marketing, media and copywriting, photography and image repair.

In the coming year, I'm hoping to add video editing, web analytics and interface design to the list.

 

Education

I'm currently a second year student of Creative Multimedia, a course run by Limerick School of Art & Design in Ireland.

I've gained exposure to many graphic softwares, including the Adobe Suite, as well as Audacity, Reaper, and Brackets.

Language-wise, I'm comfortable with ActionScript and Javascript, as well as common Javascript and CSS frameworks.

Outside of college, I read books about art and film, look at art (sometimes about books/films) and watch films (often based upon art/books).

 

Principles

I aim to create web applications that are pretty, performant and a joy to use, following the KISS principle.

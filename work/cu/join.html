Become a member

Joining Cahir Credit Union is the best decision you could take for your money. We accept new members of all ages who are living or working within a ten-mile radius of Cahir.

Member Benefits

  • Members can take out flexible loans once they have saved regularly for 3 consecutive months. The loan repayments will be structured to suit the member so that it is affordable. All our loans are fully insured, against disability or death, subject to policy requirements
  • Interest is charged on the reducing loan balance - you only pay interest on what you owe. As your loan reduces so does your interest which means lower interest charges paid over the life of the loan
  • In 2015 we approved a 25% Interest rebate on loan interest paid by its members. This rebate is refunded back into the member's shares.
  • In 2015 we paid 0.5% Dividend and in 2014 we paid 0.75% Dividend on members' savings. These savings are also insured in our Life Insurance scheme at no additional cost to the member
  • Ease of payment — you can phone our office with your debit card details and pay up to a max of €500 to your savings or loan
  • No penalties are charged on early repayment of loans. If you want, you can pay more than the agreed repayment amount and you will clear the loan faster!
  • Loans operate on a reducing balance so as your loan reduces so does the interest
  • Credit Union Interest rates are fair and reasonable and capped by Law
  • Your savings are protected under the Government's Deposit Protection Scheme up to a maximum of €100,000 per member
  • You can be assured of a professional service from Cahir Credit Union with no transaction charges or hidden fees
  • Arrange a Standing Order with your bank and be sure of regular payments to your Credit Union account. We will send you a statement quarterly showing all transactions

 

How do I become a member?

In order to apply for membership you must present yourself at the Credit Union office with the following documentation:

  1. Valid Photo I.D. (e.g. Passport or Driving Licence)
  2. Proof of address (e.g. Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Credit Card Statement dated within 3 months)
  3. Proof of PPS number (PPSN Card or any correspondence with PPSN number quoted on it)
  4. Minimum initial lodgment of €10
Once all the documentation is acceptable one of our staff will be happy to take you through the account opening process.

 

Minor Accounts (U16's)

Whether it be opening an account for your new baby or getting your school going child to start saving, having a credit union account is one of the most practical things that you/they can do for their future. You never know, they might need that loan for college down the road!

We have a standing order facility that makes saving easy and before you know it your child's savings will have accumulated into a nice lump sum for their future financial needs.

There is no age restriction to becoming a member of a credit union, as long as the applicant meets the Common Bond criteria i.e. living or working within a ten mile radius.

However, if a member is under 16, they do not have the right to:

  1. Vote
  2. Nominate property
  3. Become a member of the Board or a member of any committee

Application Process (for minor accounts)
The parent/guardian needs to bring the following:

  • Valid Photo I.D. (e.g. Passport or Driving Licence)
  • Proof of address (e.g. Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Credit Card Statement dated within 3 months)
  • Proof of PPS number (PPSN Card or any correspondence with PPSN number quoted on it)
  • Minimum initial lodgment of €1
  • Proof of Identity for child (e.g. Birth Certificate, passport)
  • PPSN number for child (PPSN Card or any correspondence with PPSN number on it)

Withdrawals Process (for minor accounts)
In order for a child of 7 and over to withdraw from their savings the child and the authorised parent (the parent who initially set up the account) must both be present and sign for withdrawals.

For children under 7 the authorised parents signature will suffice on its own, the child is not required to be present.
